Summary

Something I'm always thinking about is the meaning and purpose of prayer. I constantly ponder, "Why do I pray?" Elaine S. Dalton stated, though she directed her words to the women, they apply to all, that prayer can bring peace to a troubled soul. An interesting thought is that sometimes we say that all prayers will be answered, or we say that all righteous prayers will be answered. It's important to note that the apostles and leaders of the church have taught that all our prayers will be heard, and this is the truth we should focus on. When our timetables conflict with those of the Lord, it could lead us to question, but when we know that our prayers are being heard, we can be at ease.
