Summary

Teaching with love can be one of the greatest skills we can learn in this life. Often, it is hard to imagine how the Savior taught since we know that he was perfect. But Jesus Christ didn't use some secret way of teaching. He simply taught with love and truth. Jesus was a speaker of truth, which allowed him to inspire people to be better, but he loved them in a way that many were able to accept his teachings with the desire to change. As we teach others, we can express gratitude for what the Lord has accomplished. Teaching with love and truth doesn't mean that we have answers to every question. Sharon Eubank shares, "He asks us to do what we can and what we should and what we are assigned."
