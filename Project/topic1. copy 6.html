Summary

Meekness may often be mistakenly defined as similar to weakness, but as Elder Bednar states, being meek means to be strong, for Jesus Christ was meek. We have a record of the perfect example of meekness in our grasp. Jesus Christ came to the Earth to do the will of His Father. In the Garden of Gethsemane, Jesus Christ pleaded that the Lord would remove the cup from Him but only according to His will. Jesus was willing to submit to the will of the Father over anything else, including the saving of His own life. Truly, as President Nelson says, this attribute is the hallmark of a disciple of Jesus Christ.
